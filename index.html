The Systems Behind the Song

And we’re sharing everything that brought “I Am Feeling Like a Pen” to life ~ the Blender files, the Codebase, the whole process. Open source.

As a computer science student, I’ve learned what it means to build something open-source. Some of the visuals of the video were written/programmed line by line in p5.js.

This museum of work exists because the idea mattered. And now that it’s real, we don’t want to keep it to ourselves.
